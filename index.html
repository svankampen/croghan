Brand Values

Sustainability

We work with brands that believe in sustainability. We believe a product should last a life time and offer the best value for our customers in terms of longevity.

Giving Back

We believe an in a world where we look after one another. Thats why Croghan is part of the 1% club, 1% of all of our profits are donated to charity

Highest Quality

Products stocked by Croghan are tried and tested by our team. We only stock products after they have stood up to harsh testing. Our range is designed to be used heavily.

The Experience

At Croghan we believe in the cooking and dining experience. Cooking should be something to get excited about from how each pan feels in your hand to the aroma of the candle set at the table awaiting your guests.
