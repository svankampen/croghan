About Us

Croghan was created by two brothers with passion for the cooking and dining experience

Sam van Kampen

Sam van Kampen is a Co - Founder of Croghan Homeware. His background is in Business Analytics with an Honours Degree in Business Management and is both LEAN and Prince2 Certified. He enjoys spending time with his family, hiking, endurance training, playing the guitar and generally making the most of life. Sam has completed the Hell and Back Challenge and holds a 1st Degree Black Belt in Taekwondo.

Matt van Kampen

Matt van Kampen is a Co - Founder of Croghan Homeware. He has over 15 years’ experience in Brand Management & Business Development. Recently Matt has graduated with a Higher Diploma in “High Performance Sales & Business Development “from the Smurfit Business School. Outside of the office Matt follows, what he calls the “Three F’s” Food with Family and Friends! He enjoys travelling and has a passion for culinary art.
